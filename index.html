The Punisher

Marvel's The Punisher, or simply The Punisher, is an American web television series created for Netflix by Steve Lightfoot, based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name. It is set in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and is a spin-off of Marvel's Daredevil.The series revolves around Frank Castle, who uses lethal methods to fight crime as the vigilante "the Punisher". Jon Bernthal reprises the role as Castle from Daredevil.
